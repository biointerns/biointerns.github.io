**Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology Research Internship Program**

_Open to students around the world_

**Where**: Okinawa, Japan.

**When**: 3 to 6 months (to be decided with Group Leader) between 01/10/2026 and 31/03/2027 for autumun 2026 internship 

**Application Deadline**: 15/04/2026 for autumn 2026 internship

**Benefits**: accommodation and travel are covered + 2,400 JPY allowance per working day for personal expenses

**Website**: [OIST internship](https://www.oist.jp/admissions/research-internship/program-description)
